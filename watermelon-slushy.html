Watermelon-slushy

Nothing beats the taste of cold watermelon on a sunny day.
This is a simple and quick recipe to create a watermelonie drink.

Ingredients:
  • Watermelon
How to:
  1. Put the watermelon on a hard surface.
  2. grab a hammer
  3. Beat the watermelon until the pieces have the prefered size
  4. Scrape the juice and pieces into a jar or glas
  5. Add a straw and the drink i finished
