Toast

A toast in the morning makes for a perfect and non-nutricious breakfast.
Below I'll describe how it's created.

Ingredients:
  • A piece of toast
  • Butter
How to:
  1. Put the toast in the toaster
  2. Start toaster
  3. Wait until toaster is done...
  4. Put the toast on a plate. We don't want crumbs all over the place.
  5. Smere butter on the toast
  6. Extra: add prefered topping. May I suggest cheese
  7. Enjoy your toast
