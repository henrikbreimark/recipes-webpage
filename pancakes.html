Pancakes

Pancakes is every childs favourite making knowing how to cook them a necessity for every parent
The following recipe descripes how to make simple and delicious pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 6 dl of milk
  • 2.5 dl of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Butter

How to:

  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl
  2. Add 3 dl of milk in the bowl
  3. Whisk it togheter
  4. Add the flour and whisk
  5. Add the rest of the milk
  6. Whisk it togheter until to batter is smooth
  7. Add butter in a frying pan and heat it up
  8. Poor about half a dl of the batter into the frying pan
  9. When the batter is stiff, flip it over
  10. After about a minute place the finished pancake on a plate
  11. Serve with jam and cream
